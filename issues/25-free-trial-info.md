# Y2Pal PDF Splitter Free Trial — What's Included

Y2Pal PDF Splitter offers a free tier that serves as an open trial. No credit card, no sign-up, no email confirmation. Here's what you get, what's capped, and how to decide whether to upgrade.

## Free Tier — What You Can Do

- Split PDFs by page range, bookmark, or every N pages.
- Process PDFs up to the free-tier size cap (shown at the page header).
- Up to the daily operation count (shown at the page header).
- Use all 5 UI languages.
- Process encrypted PDFs with open-password.
- Download output files individually or as a zip bundle.
- All output is unwatermarked.

## Free Tier — What's Capped

- **File size**: The maximum source file size for free uploads, displayed on the page.
- **Daily operations**: A per-day count of split jobs, resetting at UTC midnight.
- **Concurrent sessions**: One active session at a time per device/browser.
- **Priority queue**: Free tier uses the standard queue; large files may wait longer.

## What the Free Tier Does *Not* Do

- Does **not** watermark output files.
- Does **not** reduce output quality.
- Does **not** limit the number of output files per split operation — only the number of split *operations* per day is counted.
- Does **not** require an email or account.

## When the Free Tier Is Enough

- Occasional ad-hoc splitting — splitting a single PDF once per day or a few times per week.
- Small-to-medium source files under the free cap.
- Individual use rather than team/workflow use.

## When to Upgrade to Lifetime License

- Daily operation count regularly reached.
- Regularly processing source PDFs larger than the free cap.
- Multi-session use (split from laptop and phone simultaneously).
- Want priority queue for faster processing on large files.

## How to Purchase

Visit [y2pal.com/split-pdf](https://y2pal.com/split-pdf) and click the upgrade link in the page header. Payment is a one-time charge — there is no recurring subscription. After purchase, the license is tied to your email and unlocks on any device where you sign in.
